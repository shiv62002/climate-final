This interactive heatmap allows you to explore how economic and climate metrics have changed throughout the world from 2000 to 2024. Five datasets can be selected to show year-by-year trends for every country:

GDP Growth (%)
Wheat Yield (tons/hectare)
Temperature Anomaly (¬∞C)
Consumer Price Inflation (%)
Disaster Damages (% of GDP)

Both data sets are represented on the global maps with color density reflecting the value of the selected indicator for a given year. On hover, a nation shows data for the corresponding year. Clicking on a nation opens up the dashboard of the selected country, showing historical trends (2000-2024) for the five indicators.

The visualization is used to illustrate the relationship between economic performance and climate influences, allowing the user to investigate country-level climate-economic relationships.
